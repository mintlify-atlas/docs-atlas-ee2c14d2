Edpuzzle Answers is a browser bookmarklet that gives you a set of powerful tools for any Edpuzzle assignment — no login, no browser extension installation, and no account required. Run it once on an assignment page and a popup appears with answers, controls to skip the video, and options to auto-submit everything automatically.

## What it does

When you click the bookmarklet on an Edpuzzle assignment, it opens a popup that:

- **Shows all answers** — Multiple-choice answers are highlighted for every question in the assignment
- **Auto-submits answers** — Clicks through and submits every correct answer automatically
- **Skips the video** — Marks the video as fully watched without you having to sit through it
- **Controls playback speed** — Forces the video to play at any speed (works on Edpuzzle, YouTube, and Vimeo embeds)
- **Answers open-ended questions** — Uses AI (Google Gemini) to generate and submit free-response answers
- **Prevents auto-pause** — Keeps the video playing even when you switch to another browser tab

## Quick setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the landing page">
    Navigate to [edpuzzle.hs.vc](https://edpuzzle.hs.vc) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the bookmarklet">
    Drag the button on that page into your bookmarks bar (Chrome/Edge/Brave/Opera), or right-click it and choose **Bookmark link** (Firefox).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install a CSP bypass extension">
    Due to Edpuzzle's Content Security Policy, you need a browser extension to allow the script to run. Install the appropriate one for your browser — see [Installation](/installation) for links.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run it on an assignment">
    Navigate to any Edpuzzle assignment and click your new bookmark. The answer popup will open automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The bookmarklet works on public Edpuzzle assignments only. Private assignments where the teacher has restricted access cannot be answered — the underlying data is not available without teacher credentials.
</Note>
